About This Application

🤖 Technology

This application uses Multinomial Naive Bayes with TF-IDF vectorization to classify messages as spam or legitimate (ham).

📊 Performance

  • Accuracy: 98.5%
  • Precision: 98.9%
  • Recall: 95.4%
  • F1-Score: 97.1%

📚 Dataset

Trained on the SMS Spam Collection Dataset containing 5,574 labeled messages.

🎯 Risk Levels

  • HIGH - Spam probability > 70%
  • MODERATE - Spam probability 30-70%
  • LOW - Spam probability < 30%
